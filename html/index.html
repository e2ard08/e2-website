Where I'm From?

I was born in Caracas, Venezuela in 1988, and I lived in that beautiful country for 29 years. Venezuela holds a special place in my heart, with its rich culture, stunning landscapes, and warm people.

One of the things I absolutely adore about Venezuela is its marvelous beaches. The country boasts some of the most breathtaking coastal areas I've ever seen. The crystal-clear waters and white sandy shores are a true paradise for beach lovers like me.

And let's not forget about the food – Venezuelan cuisine is simply unparalleled. From delicious arepas to mouthwatering empanadas, I savored every bite of the unique flavors and dishes that Venezuela had to offer.

But what truly makes Venezuela stand out is the incredible hospitality of its people. The warmth and friendliness of Venezuelans made me feel at home wherever I went. Their welcoming nature and willingness to share their culture and traditions left a lasting impression on me.

In January 2018, I made the decision to move to Argentina in search of new opportunities and a fresh start. I embarked on a journey to this vibrant nation where I have continued to cherish my Venezuelan roots. Argentina has welcomed me with open arms, and I've come to love its wonderful beaches, unparalleled cuisine, and the incredible hospitality of its people. The magnificent beaches, the incomparable food, and the remarkable warmth of the people have made my experience in Argentina truly special.

I arrived in Buenos Aires, Argentina, in January 2018, and I had the opportunity to explore this incredible country and immerse myself in its rich culture.

During my time there, I got to know many fabulous places. I was truly enchanted by the passion for soccer, the genuine feeling of having a friend on "Día del Amigo," the delicious alfajores, the succulent meat, the flavorful Beer, and so much more.

Argentina's warmth and hospitality left a lasting impression on me, and I carry those memories with me as I embark on my new journey in the United States.

in June 2021, I arrived in Orlando, Florida, a truly marvelous city known for its vibrant culture and diverse attractions. From its world-renowned theme parks to its picturesque lakes and beautiful beaches, Orlando offered an array of experiences that enriched my life.

However, in 2023, I made the decision to relocate to Omaha, Nebraska. This state in the heart of the country presented a different perspective, more aligned with my personal goals. In Nebraska, particularly in Omaha, I found a sense of focus and purpose that resonated with me.

One remarkable aspect of Nebraska is the experience of all four seasons throughout the year. The changing landscapes and weather patterns have added a unique dimension to my life, allowing me to appreciate the beauty of each season in its own way. My journey has taken me from the enchanting warmth of Florida to the heartland of Nebraska, where I continue to grow and pursue my aspirations.
